Eligibility

You’ll qualify if either:

  • you’re at least 10 weeks pregnant
  • you have at least 1 child under 4 years old

You must also be receiving any of the following benefits:

  • Income Support
  • income-based Jobseeker's Allowance
  • income-related Employment and Support Allowance
  • Child Tax Credit (but only if your family’s annual income is £16,190 or less)
  • Universal Credit (but only if your family earns £408 or less per month from employment)
  • Pension Credit

If you do not receive any benefits you can apply if you’re:

  • under 18 years old and pregnant
  • under 20 years old, pregnant and in full-time education
