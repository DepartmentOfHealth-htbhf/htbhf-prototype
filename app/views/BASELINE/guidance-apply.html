Apply for Healthy Start

! Warning You can only apply if your benefit has been approved.

It only takes about 5 minutes to apply.

Before you apply you will need your:

  • National Insurance number
  • baby’s due date if you’re pregnant
  • contact details which should match those registered with the benefits agencies (if you claim benefits)
  • mobile telephone number
  • email address

You need to apply over the phone if you’re:

  • under 18 years old and pregnant
  • under 20 years old, pregnant and in full-time education

Call:

  • 0300 330 7010
  • select option 3
