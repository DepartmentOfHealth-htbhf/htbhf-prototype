Agents

Data files

Data files that we send are unencrypted. Your organisation is responsible for storing them securely.

When your organisation sends a data file to us, it must:

  • have already been agreed with HMRC
  • follow the naming standards - you’ll be told about them by the HMRC department you deal with

  • not be an executable (.exe) file

Any data files your organisation sends to us must be complete. We must be able to be view them.

If they’re incomplete or we can’t view them, your obligation to provide information may not be satisfied. We’ll let you know as soon as possible if this happens, but we can’t guarantee to let you know in time to avoid penalties.

We can’t accept any liability that comes from your failure to keep us informed about authority changes.

Security

Your organisation must not use the service for data that’s classified as Secret in line with Government Security Classifications unless:

  • it has already been agreed in advance with us
