Update your details for the help to buy healthy food scheme

What to update

Your help to buy healthy food entitlement (the amount of money you get each month) depends on a number of different things. That is why you must tell us when you:

  • are pregnant
  • give birth

You do not need to let us know if your job or income changes. We will automatically be told this by other government departments.

change address or contact details, move in with your partner, stop living with your partner??????

How to tell us about a change

You can use this online form to tell us about a change in your life. It takes around 5 minutes to complete.

Start now

Before you start

You can also update your details by calling the Healthy Start helpline on 0345 607 6823. You can find call charge information on GOV.UK.

To update your details online or by telephone you will need your:

  • full name
  • date of birth
  • National Insurance number
  • baby’s due date (if you are pregnant)
  • baby's birth date (if you recently gave birth)
  • postal address
  • mobile phone number
  • email address

If you live with a partner, you will also need your partner’s:

  • full name
  • date of birth
  • National Insurance number

Your details must match the information you gave us when you applied for any other benefits.
