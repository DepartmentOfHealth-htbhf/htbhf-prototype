Apply for the help to buy healthy food scheme

If you currently get Healthy Start vouchers you need to use this online form to move to the help to by healthy food scheme.

You can check if you are eligible for this scheme before you apply.

To apply online you will need your:

  • full name
  • date of birth
  • National Insurance number
  • baby’s due date (if you are pregnant)
  • postal address
  • mobile phone number
  • email address

If you live with a partner, you will also need your partner’s:

  • full name
  • date of birth
  • National Insurance number

If you do not have a National Insurance number, please visit this GOV.UK help page.

Your details must match the information you gave when you applied for any other benefits.

Applying online takes around 5 minutes.

Start now

You can also apply by telephone

You can also register by calling the Healthy Start helpline on 0345 607 6823. You can find call charge information on GOV.UK.
