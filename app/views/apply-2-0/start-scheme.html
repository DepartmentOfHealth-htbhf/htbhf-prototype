Overview

The help to buy healthy food scheme supports pregnant women and young families on benefits. You can find out if you are eligible here.

If you are eligible, you will get a minimum of Â£12.40 each month to spend on fresh or frozen fruit and vegetables, milk and infant formula milk. You can also claim free vitamins.

We will send you a special prepaid card (like a bank card) by post. We will add your money to the card every 4 weeks ready for you to spend. You can find out how to shop with your prepaid card here.

How to apply for the first time

If you have never applied for help to buy healthy food or the Healthy Start scheme, you can apply here.

How to move from Healthy Start

If you are currently on the Healthy Start scheme and received a letter about moving to help to buy healthy food, you should fill in this online form.

This scheme is only available if you live in England, Wales or Northern Ireland. If you live in Scotland, visit the Scottish Government website for information.
