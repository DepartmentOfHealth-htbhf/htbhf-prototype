Check if you are eligible for the help to buy healthy food scheme

Who can apply

You can apply if you are under the age of 18 and at least 10 weeks pregnant, even if you do not get any benefits.

You can apply if you are over the age of 18 and at least 10 weeks pregnant, or have a child under the age of four years old, and you or your family get:

  • Income Support, or
  • Income-based Jobseeker’s Allowance, or
  • Income-related Employment and Support Allowance, or
  • Child Tax Credit (with a family income of £16,190 or less per year)
  • Universal Credit (with a family take home pay of £408 or less per month)

Who cannot apply

If you are under the age of 16 your parent, guardian or carer must apply for you.

This scheme is only available if you live in England, Wales or Northern Ireland. If you live in Scotland, visit the Scottish Government website for information.
