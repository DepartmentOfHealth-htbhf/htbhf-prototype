Report a change

You must always report a change in your circumstances or personal details. This includes a:

  • name change
  • change of contact details
  • new pregnancy

If you’re claiming any benefits you must also tell the benefit agencies about these changes.

! Warning If you deliberately do not report changes to the benefit agencies, you’re committing benefit fraud.

What you’ll need

Make sure you have your:

  • National Insurance number
  • baby’s due date (if reporting a pregnancy)
  • mobile phone if you’re updating your number
  • access to your email if you’re updating your email address
