Eligibility

You must:

  • be at least 10 weeks pregnant
  • have a child aged 3 years old or younger

You must also be receiving any of the following:

  • Income Support
  • income-based Jobseeker's Allowance
  • income-based Jobseeker's Allowance
  • Child Tax Credit (but only if your family’s annual income is £16,190 or less)
  • Universal Credit (but only if your family earns £408 or less per month from employment)
  • State Pension Credit
  • Working Tax Credit >

You’ll also be able to apply even if you do not receive any benefits and you’re:

  • under 18 and pregnant
  • under 20, pregnant and in full-time education
