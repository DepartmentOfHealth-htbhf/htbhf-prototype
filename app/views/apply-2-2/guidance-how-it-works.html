How it works

If you’re pregnant or have a child of 3 or under you could get money off baby milk, fruit, vegetables and vitamins.

If you qualify you’ll be sent a card topped up with money that you can use in UK shops.

If you live in Scotland you must apply for Best Start instead

You usually qualify if you also get certain benefits. If you’re not on benefits you could still qualify if you’re:

  • under 18 and pregnant
  • under 20, pregnant and in full-time education

You can get NHS advice on healthy eating during pregnancy, birth and parenthood at Start 4 Life.

If you’re already on the scheme

You will no longer receive vouchers. You need to apply for a Healthy Start prepaid card instead.

It only takes 5 minutes to apply online and you no longer need a signature from your midwife or health visitor.

Check the new rules about how to use your card and when it will be topped up.
