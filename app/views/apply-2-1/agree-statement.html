Submitting your application

You confirm that the information you’ve given is correct.

You understand that the Department of Health and Social Care (DHSC):

  • can check with other organisations that the information you’ve given is correct
  • can stop your payments and ask you to repay the money if you apply for a prepaid card illegally

Using your card

If you do not spend your payment within 8 weeks, your payments will be paused. You must spend the full amount for payments to start again. You will not get any payments you have missed.

You understand that:

  • you must only use your prepaid card to buy eligible items (link opens in a new tab)
  • you’re responsible for all spending on your prepaid card even if someone else uses it
  • the DHSC has the right to ask retailers for receipts when you shop – to help prevent fraud

Lost or stolen cards

If your card is lost or stolen you must report it to the card provider immediately. If not you may lose some of the money left on the card.

A small fee may be taken from your card if you request more than 4 replacement cards in a year. This is to cover costs.

About your personal information

If your circumstances or personal details change you must tell:

  • the DHSC and the Help to buy healthy food service
  • any other benefit agencies you claim from

You must read and agree to our privacy statement (link opens in a new tab). This explains what happens to your information, where it will be stored it and who it will be shared with.
