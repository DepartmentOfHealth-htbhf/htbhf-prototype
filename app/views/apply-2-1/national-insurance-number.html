What is your National Insurance number?

It is on your National Insurance card, benefit letter, payslip or P60. For example, ‘QQ 12 34 56 C’.
Why do we need this?
We need to know your National Insurance number to check if you are eligible for this scheme. If you do not have a National Insurance number, please visit GOV.UK (link opens in a new tab) for help.
