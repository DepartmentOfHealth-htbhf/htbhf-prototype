How to buy healthy food and milk with a prepaid card

Eligible pregnant women and young families can get money to buy healthy food and milk. You can check if you are eligible here.

How much money you and your family get
Pregnant women £3.10 each week
Child under the age of 1 £6.20 each week
Child aged 1 - 4 £3.10 each week

If you are eligible, we will send you a special prepaid card (like a bank card) by post. We will add your money to the card every 4 weeks ready for you to spend.

What you can buy

You can only spend this money on:

  • milk
  • plain fresh or frozen fruit and vegetables
  • infant formula milk

Plus you will get coupons for free vitamins too.

Where you can use your prepaid card

You can use your prepaid card to buy healthy food from most shops, retailers and market stalls that accept credit and debit cards.

You cannot use your prepaid card to buy things online.
