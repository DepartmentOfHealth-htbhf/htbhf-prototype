Report a change in circumstances for the help to buy healthy food scheme

Changes you need to report

Your help to buy healthy food entitlement (the amount of money you get each month) depends on a number of different things. That is why you must tell us when you:

  • are pregnant
  • give birth

We need your contact details for security checks, and to let you know about important information. So you must also tell us when you change your:

  • name
  • address
  • mobile phone number
  • email address

You do not need to let us know if your job or income changes. We will automatically be told this by other government departments.

How to report a change

You can report a change using our online form. It will take around 5 minutes to complete.

You will need your:

  • full name
  • date of birth
  • National Insurance number
  • baby’s due date (if you are pregnant)
  • postal address
  • mobile phone number
  • email address

Before you start

You can also update your details by calling the Healthy Start helpline on 0345 607 6823. You can find call charge information on GOV.UK.
