Cookies

Cookies are files saved on your phone, tablet or computer when you visit a website.

We use cookies to store information about how you use the Apply for Healthy Start website.

Cookie settings

We use 2 types of cookies.

Cookies that measure website use

We use Google Analytics to measure how you use the website. This is so we can improve it based on user needs.
