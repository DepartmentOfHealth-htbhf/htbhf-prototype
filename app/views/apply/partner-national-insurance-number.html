What is your partner’s National Insurance number?

It is on their National Insurance card, benefit letter, payslip or P60. For example ‘QQ 12 34 56 C’.
I do not have my partner's National Insurance number
We need to know your partner's National Insurance number to check if you are eligible for Healthy Start vouchers. If you do not have a National Insurance number, please visit GOV.UK (link opens in a new tab) for help.
