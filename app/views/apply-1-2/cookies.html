Cookies

The service currently known as help to buy healthy food will put small files known as 'cookies' onto your computer.

These cookies are used to help us:

  • keep track of the information you give us during your application
  • remember which language you want to view the service in
  • understand how you use the service, so we can make improvements

Find out how to manage cookies on the Information Commissioners' website.

Google Analytics cookie

We use Google Analytics to collect information about how you use the service. This information helps us to make improvements.

The Google analytics cookie collects and stores information about:

  • the pages you visit - how long you spend on each page
  • how you got to the service
  • what you click on while you are using the service
Name Purpose Expires
_ga Used to identify unique users and inform referring sites, visitor and session counts. 2 years
_gid Used to identify unique users and inform referring sites, visitor and session counts. 24 hours
_gat_UA-133839203_4 Used to protect the Google Analytics server by limiting the number of requests being made. 1 minute

Google is not allowed to use or share our analytics data with anyone.

Session cookies

We store session cookies on your computer to remember your information and keep it secure while you use the service.

Name Purpose Expires
htbhf.sid Used for session management. 30 minutes after you open each service webpage.
lang Remember your choice of language. 30 minutes after you open each service webpage.
