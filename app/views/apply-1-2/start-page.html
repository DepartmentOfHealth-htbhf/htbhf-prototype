Apply for free healthy food and milk for pregnant women and young families

Who can apply

You can apply if you are under the age of 18 and at least 10 weeks pregnant, even if you do not get any benefits.

You can apply if you are over the age of 18 and at least 10 weeks pregnant, or have a child under the age of four years old, and you or your family get:

  • Income Support, or
  • Income-based Jobseeker’s Allowance, or
  • Income-related Employment and Support Allowance, or
  • Child Tax Credit (with a family income of £16,190 or less per year)
  • Universal Credit (with a family take home pay of £408 or less per month)

Who cannot apply

If you are under the age of 16 your parent, guardian or carer must apply for you.

This scheme is only available if you live in England, Wales or Northern Ireland. If you live in Scotland, visit the Scottish Government website for information.

Applying takes around 5 minutes.

Before you start

You can also register by calling the Healthy Start helpline on 0345 607 6823. You can find call charge information on GOV.UK.

To apply online or by telephone you will need your:

  • full name
  • date of birth
  • National Insurance number
  • baby’s due date (if you are pregnant)
  • postal address

If you live with a partner, you will also need your partner’s:

  • full name
  • date of birth
  • National Insurance number

If you do not have a National Insurance number, please visit this GOV.UK help page.

Your details must match the information you gave us when you applied for any other benefits. You can find out why and how we will process your data by reading our privacy notice.
