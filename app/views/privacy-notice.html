Privacy notice

The Apply for Healthy Start service is operated by the Department of Health and Social Care (DHSC).

DHSC is the data controller of your personal data (information). A data controller determines how and why data is processed. We need to process your data to operate the Apply for Healthy Start service.

You can read the DHSC personal data charter for more information.

Our cookie notice explains how our service will interact with your device.

What data we collect and why we need it

Your data Why we need it
Name To check your eligibility and create your prepaid card account
National Insurance number To check your eligibility
Date of birth To check your eligibility and to create your prepaid card account
Address To check your eligibility and to send you your prepaid card
Contact details To create and activate your prepaid card account and to send you service updates
Children’s names We do not store or share the names of children under 13 years old
Children’s dates of birth To check yours and their eligibility, to confirm your identity and to prevent fraud

Children’s privacy protection

Parents or legal guardians are responsible for the personal data of your children who are under 13 years old.

By agreeing to our terms and conditions you allow us to process your children’s data in the same way as your data. Their data becomes your data.

What we do with your data

The data we collect may be shared with other relevant government departments, agencies and public bodies associated with the service.

It may also be shared with our technology suppliers, for example our hosting provider.

We will share your data if we are required to do so by law – for example, by a court order, or to prevent fraud or other crime.

We will not:

  • sell or rent your data to third parties
  • share your data with third parties for marketing purposes

Sharing your data with allpay

Your prepaid money card will be provided by allpay Limited. For this reason we must share some of your data with them.

We share all your data with them except your National Insurance number. This is so they can:

  • confirm your identity and address
  • create and send your card or replacement cards
  • send you information about any service changes

You should read allpay's privacy statement to find out what it does with your information.

How we protect your data and keep it secure

Your data will be stored on secure servers in the UK and the European Economic Area.

How long we keep your data

We will only retain your personal data for as long as:

  • we need it to deliver your payment via the Apply for Healthy Start service
  • the law requires us to

We will delete your data after this.

Children’s privacy protection

We will not collect or store data about anyone under the age of 13.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • see what data we hold about you (a right of access request)
  • ask us to stop using your data but keep it on record
  • ask to have some or all of your data deleted
  • have some or all of your data corrected

If you have any of these requests, email the Information Asset Owner at nhsbsa.applyforhealthystart@nhsbsa.nhs.uk.

If you ask us to delete your data you will no longer have access to the Apply for Healthy Start service. This means your entitlement (money) will stop.

You can apply for the Healthy Start voucher scheme instead if you choose.

Contact us or make a complaint

Contact our Data Protection Officer if you:

  • have a question about anything in this privacy notice
  • think that your personal data has been misused or mishandled
